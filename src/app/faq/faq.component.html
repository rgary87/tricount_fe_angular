F.A.Q

Why shouldn't I refund if some spendings haven't yet been added? Even if they do not concerns me?

Short Answer: Because of the way the distribution algorithm works.

Long Answer: When the distribution is done, some sorting and ordering happens in the list of participants and spendings. But, as you can expect, creating a refund with change these sorting and ordering. After all, a refund is nothing more than a spending from one participant concerning only one other participant.



But wait! We missed a spending, created it late, after some refund started, does that mean we should return the money and start again?!
Don't worry! The system is designed for this kind of event. In this case, the distribution will transform any refunds marked as done in a spending and will take it into account during the distribution process.
Some participant may need to refund again another amount, some may see their, previously needed, refund disappear. It all depends on who did what and for how much.
